About BTUG

An independent home for Bengaluru's data community

The Bengaluru Tableau User Group is a volunteer-led community for analysts, BI developers and data enthusiasts who want to learn, share and grow with Tableau and modern analytics.

Your one-stop destination

This is your one-stop destination for everything related to the Bengaluru Tableau User Group. Here you will find information and resources designed to help you learn, connect and grow as part of the Tableau community.

This group is exclusively for Bengaluru Tableau User Group members interested in expanding their Tableau skills and engaging with the local analytics community.

What you'll find here

  • Updates on upcoming BTUG events, meetups and community sessions
  • Session recordings, presentation materials and shared learning resources
  • Announcements about workshops, trainings and special Tableau initiatives
  • Community highlights, speaker sessions and member-led discussions

Our mission

To build one of India's strongest Tableau and analytics communities — a welcoming, high-signal space where Bengaluru's data people level up through practice, mentorship and real connection.

Our vision

A thriving, inclusive data community where every member, from curious beginner to seasoned expert, finds the people, knowledge and opportunities to do their best work with data.

What we stand for

Our core values

  • Learn

    Grow real Tableau and analytics skills through hands-on sessions and peer mentoring.

  • Share

    Openly exchange techniques, datasets and dashboards — knowledge multiplies when shared.

  • Inspire

    Spotlight community work and data stories that spark new ideas and ambition.

  • Collaborate

    Build together across companies, roles and experience levels.

  • Grow Together

    Lift the whole community, from first-time learners to seasoned practitioners.

A note on independence

BTUG is an independent, community-run group and is not the official Tableau (Bevy) user-group chapter. We are organized by volunteers for the love of data. For the official Tableau User Group chapter for Bengaluru, please visit the official chapter page.
