What we do

A community built to learn and grow together

BTUG runs year-round programming for analysts, BI developers and data enthusiasts across Bengaluru.

  • ◆

    Workshops

    Hands-on, laptop-open sessions on calculations, LOD, performance and dashboard design.

  • ◇

    Meetups

    Regular community gatherings with speakers from across Bengaluru’s data ecosystem.

  • ◈

    Hackathons

    Time-boxed dashboard challenges that turn raw datasets into compelling data stories.

  • ▦

    Conferences

    Our flagship event bringing together keynotes, labs, AI sessions and a sponsor expo.
