Volunteer with BTUG

BTUG runs entirely on volunteers. Hosting an evening, mentoring at a workshop or running our socials directly shapes how Bengaluru's data community learns and connects.

Why volunteering matters

Every meetup, workshop and conference you have enjoyed was made possible by members who gave their time. Volunteering is how you grow your network, sharpen new skills and give back — and it is the single biggest reason BTUG keeps going year after year.

A note on sign-ups

Sign-ups go through a Google Form that collects your email. Because this is a static site with no backend, we cannot automatically block a second submission — so please sign up once per role. Organisers de-duplicate entries by email before confirming, and a role stops showing its sign-up button as soon as it is filled.
