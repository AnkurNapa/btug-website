Spend a focused Saturday morning turning messy spreadsheets into an
analysis-ready data set with **Tableau Prep Builder**. This is a hands-on,
laptop-open session — you build alongside the facilitator, step by step, and
walk out with a flow you have published yourself.

No prior Prep experience is needed. If you can open a CSV, you are ready.

## You will learn

- Joins, unions, and aggregations in Prep
- Cleaning steps, pivots, and calculated fields
- Publishing a flow output to Tableau Server
