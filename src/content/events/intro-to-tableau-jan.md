Our **January kickoff** welcomed over fifty first-time Tableau users for a
hands-on introduction to building their first visualisations.

## Recap

We covered connecting to data, the shelves and marks card, and building a first
dashboard from scratch. The room was buzzing — many attendees published their
first Tableau Public viz the same afternoon.

Thanks to everyone who came out, and to IIM Bangalore for hosting. Slides and the
sample workbook are available in our resources section. See you at the next one.
