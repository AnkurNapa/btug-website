About the session

Messy spreadsheets in, a published flow out

Spend a focused Saturday morning turning messy spreadsheets into an analysis-ready data set with Tableau Prep Builder. This is a hands-on, laptop-open session — you build alongside the facilitator, step by step, and walk out with a flow you have published yourself.

No prior Prep experience is needed. If you can open a CSV, you are ready.

You will learn

  • Joins, unions, and aggregations in Prep
  • Cleaning steps, pivots, and calculated fields
  • Publishing a flow output to Tableau Server

What you'll build

Three hours, three outcomes

  • 01

    Connect & combine

    Joins, unions and aggregations across messy source files.

  • 02

    Clean with confidence

    Cleaning steps, pivots, splits and calculated fields.

  • 03

    Publish a real flow

    Output an analysis-ready set and publish it to Tableau Server.

Who it's for

Good to know

Questions, answered

Do I need to bring anything?

A laptop with Tableau Prep Builder installed (a free 14-day trial is fine). We send a setup email after you register.

Is this beginner-friendly?

Yes. If you can open a CSV, you are ready. We build everything together, step by step.

What does it cost?

The workshop is free for the community. Seats are limited, so please only register if you can attend.

Is it recorded?

It is a hands-on session, so no full recording — but you keep the flow you build and the sample files.
